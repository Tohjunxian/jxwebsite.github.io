Project No. Project Name Try it! Instructions
1 TNT Clicker After the intro, press play to start the TNT clicker game. Click the TNT box to get money and press the upgrade button if you want to buy upgrades (currently 5 upgrades). Press on the upgrade to own it. If the upgrade is purchased successfully, the upgrade would say "Purchased!" and it should be working. If you have less than the required amount of money, the upgrade would not be purchased and the upgrade would say "You do not have enough money!". +4 money upgrade can be bought 4 times (for now) while auto clicker, glowing tnt box and colour change upgrade can only be bought once. Press the down arrow in the upgrade menu to go to the second page of upgrades. Click the back to clicker button if you want to go back to the game from the upgrade menu. In both computer and mobile, hover over the volume button to change the volume.
2 Christmas Tree Platformer Control your Christmas Tree player with arrow keys and reach the next level as you you hit enemies with your energy balls when you press the space key! Avoid touching the enemies and red lava that will kill you! Have fun trying to get over obstacles or avoiding lava and killing enemies!
3 Winter Santa Hurdles Use the arrow keys to move. Avoid the zombie snowmen as they drop from the sky. After you hit a snowman, you will lose a health point and the player will have a cooldown for 0.5 seconds. If you survive for 1 minute, you win. Otherwise, if you hit the snowmen 10 times and have 0 health points left, you lose and the game is over. Have fun!
4 Catch The Apples Up, down, left and right keys to move. Collect the apples. You will see a menu that asks you how many apples to catch before winning. Press the representing key and enjoy catching the apples! Press the back to menu button to go back to the menu to choose the wining points in the middle of a game. Please note: Your points will reset when going back to the menu.
5 Quiz Trivia Game Type Y when the dog asks you to play and type N to play another time. Then, select your mode (Easy, Medium, Hard or Extreme) and answer the dog's math question and try to get it correct! Check your score and encouragement at the end of the quiz! Have fun!
6 Meow At this cat's home (Heart background), you can press space and click on the cat. When you press space, the cat will move to a random position. If you click on it, it will meow. If you press on walk in the park, the cat will be at a park and you can use the arrow keys to let it walk around. Move your mouse pointer to go back home to let the cat go back to his home (Heart background).
7 Barista According to the recipe, click on the items that you need for the food/drink and it will appear in the middle. Then, you will score one point if you get it correct and lose one point if you get it wrong. More in instructions in game! Serve customers and win points! Enjoy!

9 Interactive Game 1 Choose your option according to the numbers on the option. Choose your ending! Note: This is an interactive game. No need to move anything.

11 Crazy Flying Cats Catch the cat and click it if your eyes are fast enough. It will create a clone and the clone will move crazily too. Can't press the clone to make another clone only the main cat. If you want only one note, don't press space but if you want two notes together, press space. Press P to cancel the second note. Press left arrow to delete all clones.
12 Click The Cat Just click the cat to increase the number of clicks around the world and the cat will move in a random way (to increase the difficulty). Click the green flag to change the background.
13 Tears, An Animation Just click the green flag and watch the simple and short (about 15 seconds) animation! (Updated 27/8/22) It's a story back in time about a guy with no face becoming a human thanks to a magical wizard. Enjoy!
14 Wind Bell Catcher Up, down, and left keys to move. After the intro, please quickly put your finger on the left key as the game starts immediately. Try to catch the bells to bring you back even more and try to reach the pink line to win. Otherwise, if you hit the green line, you lose. Have fun!
15 My Profile Platformer 2 Left, right and up keys to move. Avoid spikes. If you want to go over the spikes, press the up arrow first before pressing the right arrow. Reach the portal for every level to go on to the next one. Level one portal: Banana Level two portal: Lightning
16 Cat Dodge Up and down keys to move. Avoid the ball by moving up or down. If you hit the ball, you will lose. The gift will make you move two steps forward. Reach the arrow to win.
17 My Profile Platformer 1 Left, Right, and up arrows to move. Avoid spikes. If you want to go over the spikes, press the up arrow first before pressing the right arrow. Reach the portal for every level to go on to the next one. Level 1 portal: Crystal Level 2 portal: Apple
18 Lucky Coin The ultimate game of luck! After the coins shuffle and go to their positions, choose one and get your coin value (No money, 10 cents, 20 cents, 50 cents and $1)! After that, your coin will fall into 1X, 2X, 3X, 4X or a 5X multiplier! Then, check your final value at the end! Have fun getting the most amount of money!
19 Simple Claw Machine (Very Buggy) Welcome to the Simple Claw Machine! Left and right arrows to move the claw. Space to catch the ball after you aim. This is a game of luck, you may not get the ball even if you aim at the middle of it or use any skills. Have fun trying to win different prizes!
